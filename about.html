About Us

HIERARCHY GLOBAL is an avant-garde brand, with a street and punk culture that dissolves the matrix, it is about being in control of the way you dress without following what is trending with an unorthodox style that unapologetically breaks boundaries of the traditional way to dress. Conceptual, controversial and thought-provoking designs that challenge issues we face in real life and hypothetical head on. We aim to be infectious by encouraging consumers to be more expressive about the way they dress and find their own personal style within our garments to set themselves apart. Our goal globally is to promote individualism and creativity among consumers and let them choose garments that would suit their style rather than preference based on influence and trends.
